Assignment Reflection:

Before we began working on this project, we have experineced a series of group discussions to outline the basic layout for the website. We researched various websites bearing the similar theme and attempted to learn and inculcate some good ideas from their designs. After we have had meeting to generate new ideas, we finally had the ideas that we can implement into fruitful work.

We are to plan a website for a kids ice cream cafe. The dichotomy of working on a project like this is to combine two ideas at the same time. One idea is that we have to paln a website for the kids and at the same time we have to make this website easily accessible to their parents. So, our target audience for this website is both the kids, as well as their parents. It is an interesting mix of ideas and we worked hard on how to combine these two ideas in the same website. For this we used various attractive imagery to make it visually appealing to the kids, in addition to making the website more descriptive and responsive to their parents.

When the actual work on outlining the website started, we split our tasks among each other. Our website had a total of 9 pages, and we split them evenly among us to do in an efficient way. We assigned a lot of tasks for each other. One of our team members was working on the scripting, and the other team members were working on the images, text CSS at the same time. And we exchanged our roles as it suited what our needs. And at the end it was a whole team work rather than an individual work. In fact, being a part of this project has taught us the importance of working together. It was enriching experience to collaborate for all of us.

We also had usability testing, and based on that we tried to design all of the suggestions that were made during this stage of design. All the workings on the various pages in the next section.

Analysis of Usability and Accessibility Testing

According to Usability test, many groups thinks that the font style of our website is suitable. So we have tried to change a lot of time for suitable font, finally, we choose suitable font for each section.Also,in contact us page, we put google map next to text but it doesn't display location. For banner image, we want to input Diazam Cafe logo. So we changed color of Diazam Cafe with black background color with photoshop.Also, in order to caculate total cost in order page, we use javascript. For our users convenience, we put "to top" button at the end of right bottom.
