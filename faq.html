FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

ORDERING

1. How do I make a purchase?

Once you have selected your desired product, click on the 'Online Order' button and follow the instructions to complete your purchase.

2. Can I place an order over the telephone?

Please call our Customer Care team or email for advice on how to place your order.

3. What happens if I notice that my personal details are incorrect after I have completed the ordering process?

If you realise your personal details are incorrect once you have completed the ordering process please email our customer service team, with your order number.

RETURNS AND EXCHANGES

1. How do I receive a refund or exchange and how long does our Refund Policy last?

Unsuitable items may be refunded or exchanged using the same mode of payment and method of delivery as your purchase. You can request exchanges or refunds within 14 days of your purchase date.

2. When will I receive a refund for the returned items?

Refunds are processed on the day they are received and take 1-3 days to show in your account.

GENERAL QUESTIONS

1. Is my personal information kept private?

You can be assured that we consider all of the information you share with us are strictly private and confidential.

2. Can you deliver to a different address than my billing address?

First time orders must be delivered to your billing address as stated on your Credit / Debit card for verification purposes. All subsequent orders can be placed to any location you desire.
