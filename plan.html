Mission Statement

The Diazepam Café website is intended to increase profit by promoting the products and services served by the café and increase the number of repeat customers.

Success Evaluation

Other advertising will be consistent for a period of 3 months (except for including the new web site), so that any increase in sales should be attributable from the site. At the end of this 3 month period (and during), the number of new clients will be checked and compared to the past 3 months. Also, the bounce rate can be counted to check how many people are interested in knowing the website.

Target Audience

Local residents who are interested in ice cream, mostly teenagers, young adults and parents (who have young children). This will be the target audience of the website aimed.

Content

  • Home page
    • In this page, we will include some flowing pictures and upcoming event.
  • About us page
    • Information about Diazepam café, the goal of café and brief information about the founder.
  • Products page
    • In the product page, we will include all products (with pictures) which served by Diazepam café.
  • Service page
    • In this page, we will include services which provided by Diazepam café such as delivery services. We also includes the exchange and return terms and conditions.
  • Fashion Show page
    • In this page, we will include upcoming date and display interesting images to attract our clients.
  • Event page
    • In this page, we will display detail of all events with pictures.
  • F.A.Q page
    • In this page, we will include some questions which are requested from our clients frequently.
  • Contact us page
    • In this page, we will include address, contact details and email. And there is a feedback form for clients to submit their comments after visiting our website.
  • Online order page
    • In this page, we will display order form and calculate total price which clients selected.
